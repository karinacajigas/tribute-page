Milestones in Michael Phelps Career

2000

First Olympics at only 15 years old.

2001

Youngest male to set a record in swimming.

2004

Won his first gold medal in world time.

2007

Broke five world records at the World Aquatics Championships

2008

Set an all-time Olympic recdord in Beijing

2012

FINA recognized Michael as the most decorated Olympian ever.

2016

After coming back from retirement, having faced a DUI and even rehab, Phelps won multiple "gold medals at 31 years old, well beyond the typical peak for male swimmers". He was chosen as the American flag bearer and voted captain.
