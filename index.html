Desmond Doss

A war hero without weapons.

"I'm sorry, Sergeant. I can't touch a gun."

-- Desmond Doss in Hawksaw Ridge(2016)

Why did Desmond became a conscientious objector?

Desmond Doss was raised as a Seventh-day Adventist, which taught him to live according to nonviolent ways. He couldn't accept the idea of killing another human being, even at war.

Given the circumstances and his beliefs, he went to war as a combat medic.

Desmond's Accomplishments During War

During war, Desmond served at Guam, the Philippines and Okinawa. It is said that Doss helped approximately 100 wounded men.

He received the following awards for his service:

  • Medal of Honor
  • Bronze Medal
  • Purple Heart

His Legacy

Inspired by the corageous life of Desmond Doss, several creators have published works about his life. This includes four books, and two award-winning films.

Doss' story has also been featured in various TV shows and five media publications.
